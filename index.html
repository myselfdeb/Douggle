Can Humans Recognize AI-Generated Images?

AI-generated images are becoming increasingly realistic, making it challenging for humans to identify them at first glance. While subtle inconsistencies like unnatural textures or irregular features might give them away, advanced algorithms often create visuals that mimic reality flawlessly. Tools powered by AI detection can help distinguish these images, ensuring authenticity and combating misuse. The line between real and artificial is blurring, making vigilance essential
